Welcome!
I am a linguist at the Georg-August University of Göttingen. My main areas of interest are semantics/pragmatics of natural languages and philosophy of language. I am currently working as a post-doctoral researcher on a DFG-funded project "Agency Sensitivity in Grammar".

About me
I received my degree in theoretical linguistics from the University of Toronto in 2015. Since then, I have worked as a post-doctoral researcher at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Georg-August University of Göttingen, and University of Tromsø. My recent research focuses on how our intentions and sense of agency are manifested in language.
This site is a light version of a classic academic website. For more information about my education, teaching, and publications, you can take a look at my full CV. If you have any comments, requests, or questions about any of the material published here, you are welcome to contact me directly.
